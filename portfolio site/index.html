Background & Approach

Turning complexity into clarity.

I work with data to uncover what is driving performance, what is changing over time, and what actions need to be taken.

As a data analyst, my work focuses on transforming raw, inconsistent data into structured analysis that highlights patterns, trends, and key drivers of business outcomes. From high-level summaries to detailed breakdowns, I build reports that support clear and confident decisions.

I primarily use Excel and Power Query to produce analysis that is both technically sound and easy to interpret, enabling stakeholders to move from data to insight without friction.
