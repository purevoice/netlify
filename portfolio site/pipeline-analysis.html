Sales Pipeline Analysis

Excel Power Query Power BI Pipeline Analysis
Dashboard screenshot — replace with your image link in the HTML

This project involved a comprehensive analysis of the company’s sales pipeline to diagnose performance gaps, evaluate pipeline health, and provide actionable insights for improving revenue forecasting and sales effectiveness. Using Excel, Power Query, and Power BI, I transformed raw opportunity data into an interactive dashboard that visualizes deal flow, stage distribution, conversion metrics, rep performance, and pipeline coverage.

The analysis covered 1,350 opportunities with a total pipeline value of ₦549.5 million and generated ₦74.9 million in revenue. Key metrics examined include monthly sales trends, opportunity distribution by stage, active pipeline value by stage, win/loss outcomes, conversion rate (17.26%), quota attainment by sales agent, and pipeline coverage ratio (1.46x).

Major Findings

  • The pipeline is heavily front-loaded, with the largest volume and value concentrated in the early stages (Prospecting and Proposal Sent), indicating slow progression and potential qualification bottlenecks.
  • Overall conversion rate stands at 17.26%, with noticeable stage skipping (particularly from Qualified to Proposal Sent), which distorts visibility into true drop-off points.
  • Pipeline coverage is critically low at 1.46x (well below the healthy benchmark of 3–4x), signaling insufficient opportunities to meet future revenue targets even if all deals close.
  • Quota attainment is highly uneven across the sales team — only one rep exceeded 100% while the majority fell significantly short, highlighting wide performance gaps in closing ability.
  • Win/loss analysis shows the team is winning more deal value than losing, yet the low conversion rate and stalled early-stage deals continue to limit overall revenue growth.

Insights & Implications

The analysis clearly reveals that while the team is closing more deals than it loses, the combination of low pipeline coverage, early-stage congestion, and inconsistent rep performance creates substantial risk of missing quota in upcoming periods. Stage skipping and the absence of strong progression from Proposal Sent onward further reduce forecasting accuracy and sales efficiency.

This project delivered clear, data-driven visibility into the current state of the sales pipeline and equipped the sales leadership with specific, prioritized insights to guide immediate improvements in qualification processes, rep coaching, and pipeline-building activity.
